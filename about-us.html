you are Welcome

Helping People Find Their Way Back to God

At Worship & Paint, we are committed to guiding individuals on their spiritual journey. Through faith, worship, and creative expression, we help people reconnect with God, discover His purpose for their lives, and share His love with the world.

Worship – Experience His presence through vibrant worship services, both in spirit and creativity.
Creative Expression – Use art as a form of worship, painting your faith and deepening your connection to the divine.
Community – Build meaningful relationships with others who share your passion for God and creativity.
Service – Put your faith into action by serving others and living out God's love in practical ways.

We’re here to walk alongside you as you find restoration, purpose, and hope in your relationship with God. Wherever you are in life, there’s always a way back to Him.

what we believe

Living in God’s amazing grace!

At Worship & Paint, we believe that God’s grace is the foundation of our faith and the source of our hope. We are committed to living out this grace in our daily lives, reflecting His love and mercy to everyone around us. Our core beliefs include:
The Authority of Scripture – We hold the Bible as the inspired Word of God, guiding our beliefs and practices.
The Love of God – We believe that God loves every person unconditionally and desires a personal relationship with each of us.
The Power of Community – We recognize the importance of fellowship and support among believers as we grow in faith together.
The Call to Serve – We are called to serve others, using our gifts and talents to spread God’s love and make a difference in the world.
Together, we strive to live in the fullness of God’s grace, sharing that grace with others through worship, creativity, and service.

Our community was born from a deep desire to blend faith with creativity, offering a unique space where worship and artistic expression can flourish together. Our mission is to help you discover and embrace your creative gifts while nurturing your relationship with God. Here, you will find a welcoming family that encourages you to express your faith through art, connect with others, and grow spiritually. We are dedicated to reaching out to those who feel distant from God and inviting them back into His loving embrace. Through vibrant worship services, engaging small groups, and creative events, we aim to create a community that reflects God's love and grace.

I invite you to join us on this exciting journey of faith, creativity, and fellowship. Together, we can make a lasting impact in our lives and the world around us. Thank you for being a part of Worship & Paint. I can’t wait to see how God will move among us!

Worship & Paint is a community of faith committed to sharing God’s Word through creative expression and joyful worship. We believe that worship can be experienced in many forms, including painting and other artistic endeavors. At Worship & Paint, children, youth, and adults of all ages are encouraged to grow spiritually and creatively, serving the Lord through both ministry and art. You are warmly invited to join our family and discover what it's like to blend faith with creativity. Worship & Paint is a welcoming environment where everyone is valued and embraced. Our mission is to "Know Jesus and make Him known; to follow the guidance of the Holy Spirit; and to bring God's Word to the world through art and worship.
